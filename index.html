About Me

My name is Victoria Hunter. I was originally born in Shelby, NC, which is in the foothills. Because of this, I have always grown up with a love for the mountains. I decided to go somewhere further for college after high school and went to East Carolina University. I majored in Management Information Systems. This was cool because I learned about the business side of information systems, and took a couple of classes in coding. I don't have a great foundation in it though, which is why I am taking this course.

I got an internship after college which turned into a job, so for now I am in Raleigh, NC. For fun, I like to hang out with friends, do yoga, read, and travel.
